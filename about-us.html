Our Story
Welcome to Pizza Hub. The first Pizza Hub store opened in Cambridge in 2001. The revolutionary prices along with the high-quality taste we offer are a key factor of our success story. Fresh sauce and dough are made daily! You will feel the freshness in every bite. Franchising began in 2014 and currently, we are reaching 8 locations and still growing strong!
Our Working Days
Open 7 days a week 365 days a year, Pizza Hub prides itself on excellent service, quality and value using only great quality ingredients to create as well as sourcing our products. We truly believe, we make the best pizza in town and so do our customers.
