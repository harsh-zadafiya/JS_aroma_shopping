About Us

AROMA is a global fashion and lifestyle e-retailer committed to making the beauty of fashion accessible to all.
We use on-demand manufacturing technology to connect suppliers to our agile supply chain,
Our Story
We believe that the beauty of fashion should be accessible to everyone, not just the privileged few. When the company was founded in 2012, we noticed that fashion companies had to choose between offering choice or reducing inventory pressure and waste.
Making the Beauty of Fashion
Consumers no longer follow a unified standard of what is considered "fashionable" or "beautiful". We believe that the clothes we wear reflect our personalities and we want to empower everyone to explore and express their individuality.

Frequently Asked Questions

What Is AROMA?
AROMA is a community-driven app that lets you share your clothes and their stories. This way you will be able to wear new items and experiment with different styles whilst doing the planet and your wallet a favour.
How Does It Work?
Enjoy wearing the item and post a picture (“story”) on AROMA when you’re wearing it. The owner will be notified and sees your story on AROMA. Your story will be added to the item’s history on AROMA, creating a closet full of stories.
How Much Does It Cost?
As of today, AROMA can be used for free. So the perfect moment to try it out is... now! Later this year, AROMA will share its pricing model. We can only say: the more clothes you share (or “clothing stories you create”) the cheaper it will be. Because that’s the kind of world we wish to see. Stay tuned to figure out!
Will The Clothes Be Returned To The Owner?
YES! AROMA is about swapping temporarily by default. The clothes will be returned to the owner at the moment both parties have agreed upon. There is one exception: when the owner indicated the item is a ‘giveaway’ item, then the item can be kept ‘forever’ or as long as you wish
How Long Can I Borrow An Item For?
You can borrow the item for as long as both parties have agreed upon when organizing the swap. If you want to extend the borrow period of the item, make sure to contact the lender through the chat function. would like to borrow the item for longer, ask the lender via the chat function in the app.
What Is Someone Borrows My Clothes And It Is Not Returned?
When signing up to AROMA users have agreed with our Terms of Use, which state that the item must be returned in the agreed upon manner and timing decided on into the app. In case, the borrower fails to return the item (on time), we would like you to contact AROMA@AROMA.clothing we will then get in contact with the borrower, and do our utmost to get the item back to you.
